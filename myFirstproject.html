Welcome to my Jiu-Jitsu page

This is a website dedicated to my favorite Brazilian Jiu-jitsu Fighters

Roger Gracie

Roger Gracie is an 8x world brazilian jiu-jitsu Chamption. He has more 3 Abu-Dhabi and is considered the best competitive jiu-jitsu fighter.

Andre Galvao

Andre Galvao has been one of the top competitors in brazilian jiu jitsu for the last 10 years. Although he is still reletively young he has brought up many great world champions through the ranks. Two of them which are my favorite fighters.

The Mendes Brothers

The Mendes brothers are the youngest brothers to win all major championships. They started brazilian jiu-jitsu at the age of 12 and by the age of 19 won the jiu-jitsu worlds the first year they recieved their blackbelts. They are now considered the best pound for pound fighters in the world and don't look to be slowing down anytime soon!
